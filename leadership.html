Speaking Engagements

Youth Enlightenment: Student Forum on Innovation Design in Human-AI Collaboration
August 2025
Resource Speaker & Panelist

Presented “Cognitive Symbiosis: GenAI Reshaping Learning Methods and Mindsets” at Beijing Normal University during the 2025 Global Smart Education Conference. Using the Filipino idea “Bahala na si Batman,” I highlighted the risk of over-relying on AI and proposed a shift to a co-pilot model—“Let me and AI handle it together.” I introduced YourTwin, a mirror–shadow AI system for personalized cognitive growth. Represented a Gold Award entry at GCD4FE.
